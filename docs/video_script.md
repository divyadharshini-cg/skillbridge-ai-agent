# SkillBridge AI: Video Pitch Script (2 Minutes)

## Slide 1: Hook (0:00 - 0:20)
- **Visual:** SkillBridge AI banner showing "Multi-Agent Internship Readiness Coach".
- **Speaker:** "Many college students struggle to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world internships. They don't know what skills they lack, what projects to build, or how to prepare for technical interviews. Meet SkillBridge AI."

## Slide 2: The Multi-Agent Orchestration (0:20 - 0:50)
- **Visual:** System architecture flow showing the Coordinator Routing profile details.
- **Speaker:** "SkillBridge AI uses a cooperative multi-agent system. First, the Safety Agent verifies resume integrity. Next, the Profile Analyzer and Skill Gap Agents compare student skills against target roles. Finally, the Roadmap and Portfolio Agents design 30-day schedules and custom project READMEs to build out real portfolios."

## Slide 3: Live Demo Walkthrough (0:50 - 1:30)
- **Visual:** Streamlit UI tabs: Overview, Profile Integrity, Gap Analysis, 30-Day Roadmap, and Mock Interview in action.
- **Speaker:** "Through our premium dashboard, a student can select a target role like Software Engineer, upload a resume, and immediately see their matching percentage, core gaps, and a week-by-week preparation roadmap. They can also practice with mock interview questions and get evaluated instantly."

## Slide 4: Hackathon Criteria & Conclusion (1:30 - 2:00)
- **Visual:** Checklist demonstrating 90%+ readiness scores.
- **Speaker:** "By combining structured local databases, model context protocol tools, and Google Gemini API clients, SkillBridge AI helps college students step into the industry with confidence. Thank you!"
